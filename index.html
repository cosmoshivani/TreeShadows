Talk to the Trees


Every plant species has a different way of interacting with its environment. Choose the type of tree from the list or just go on and scan. All you need to do is get close to a tree, open the app, and scan. As soon as you do that, the tree signals will be converted into texts. And you can interact with the tree, ask what it's going to do next. The app can predict what the tree is doing up to a very good approximation given the time, day, season, weather, temperature, type of tree, region, color of the leaves, branch, and so on.

You can even talk to your indoor plants!






On average a fully grown tree can produce nearly 117.934 kilograms of Oxygen per year. So, by looking at the species, the health of the tree, its height, and weather conditions it's possible to predict how much oxygen its producing in real time. And not only that, you can also get all sorts of information, like what the tree is thinking, what's going on inside its roots, and how many trees it can sense around it.
